Established on a calling — 1983

"Called to Serve" — A Ministry Founded by Ndivho Brandon Tshikota

After recognizing a calling at an early age to serve the Lord in 1983, Ndivho Brandon Tshikota began a journey of faith, compassion, and discipleship. Today, Congregational Church Of All Nations continues that mission: preaching Christ, equipping believers, and serving communities.

Founder — Ndivho Brandon Tshikota

Ndivho Brandon Tshikota, moved by a divine calling in 1983 through a Vision, I saw myself been lifted up in the spirit, My feet were nolonger on the ground, as I was floating in the spirit, I saw our saviour, His arms were open while looking at me, he was wearing a blue shinning rope, I then asked, Can I come where you are? He said YES, His voice was loud than a sound of many water.I then went back, He was lifted up and I could'nt see him anymore, I find myself back on the ground, Because of my age I didn'nt know that was a call until , established this ministry to serve with humility and truth. His heartbeat is to see souls saved, believers equipped, and communities transformed by the Gospel.

He continues to lead by example — teaching the Word, mentoring leaders, and fostering unity in the Body of Christ.
